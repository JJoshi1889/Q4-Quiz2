Discover the Beauty of the Philippines

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are a masterpiece of agricultural engineering, often referred to as the eighth wonder of the world.

Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills are a geological formation in the Bohol province, and during the dry season, the grass-covered hills turn chocolate brown.

Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano is known for its near-perfect cone shape and is one of the most active volcanoes in the Philippines.
