George Orwell

Biography

George Orwell was an English novelist, essayist, journalist, and critic. He is best known for his dystopian novel 1984 and the allegorical novella Animal Farm. Orwell was a man of strong opinions who addressed some of the major political movements of his times, including imperialism, fascism, and communism, through his works.

Works

Some of George Orwell's notable works include:

Title Year
1984 1949
Animal Farm 1945
Homage to Catalonia 1938

These works are considered classics of modern literature and have had a significant impact on political thought and literature.
